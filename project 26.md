** *Vanilla Cake***

Ingredients:

1.  Flour (290g)

2.  2 teaspoons Baking powder 

3. 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda 

4. 1/2 teaspoon Salt 

5.   Butter (115g)

6.   Sugar (300g)

7.   Buttermilk  (240ml) 

8. 1 tablespoon Vanilla extract  

   For the frosting: 

   1.   Butter(150g)

   2. softened  (120ml) Heavy cream 

   3.  Icing sugar 

   4. 2 teaspoons Vanilla extract 

   5.   Cream cheese Decoration(400g):

       confetti sprinkles
